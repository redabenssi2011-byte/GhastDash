Step 3 — How to get help precisely

Text Support workflow: Navigate to #create-ticket → Click the ticket button → Describe your issue in detail → Staff will reply in your private ticket.

Voice Support workflow: Go to #voice-support → Join the voice channel → Wait for staff to join you → Share your screen if needed.

Important: Do not ping staff directly in public channels. Use the ticket system for faster and organized help.

Average response time: Text support (5-10 min) · Voice support (immediate during active hours).
